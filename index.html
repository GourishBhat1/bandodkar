    History

    Dr Bandodkar's Dental Clinic was started on 22 October 1985 in Ponda on rented premises where Hotel Sungrace now stands. It was a nice cozy single dental chair practice with Dr Dilip Bandodkar as the only dentist. His wife Dr Chhaya joined the practice in 1987.

    In 1998, we shifted to our own premises near municipal garden Ponda(Our Satellite facility at present). It is situated in the heart of Ponda city and has easy accessibility to the patients via their own vehicle and public transport as well.

    In 2008, we built our dream project - Dr Bandodkar's Dental Clinic Hilltop. Located on the Khadpabandh hill giving a picturesque view of surrounding area and unhindered flow of fresh mountain breeze. It is equipped with top of the line dental equipments for the best oral care. The premise also offers parking space for its patients. We also have our own water harvesting facility and Solar energy facility making us self sufficient.
